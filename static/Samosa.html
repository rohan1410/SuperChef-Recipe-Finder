Samosa

Ingredients
• 1½ cups flour
• 2 tablespoons oil
• ¼ teaspoon ajwain
• 2 large potatoes
• ½ cup fresh green peas
• 1 long green chilli
• ½ ginger piece
• ½ teaspoon garam masala
• ½ teaspoon coriander powder
• ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
• ⅛ teaspoon amchur
• ½ teaspoons cumin seeds
• 2 teaspoons oil
• Oil for deep frying samosa.
Method
1. To make dough:
2. Take all the ingredients in a mixing bowl. Mix well till crumbs are formed.
4. Keep it aside for 30 minutes.
5. To make potato filling:
6. Pressure or steam cook potatoes and peas until soft. Peel and coarsely mash the potatoes.
7. Crush the ginger-chilli.
8. Heat 2 teaspoons oil in a kadai.
9. Add cumin seeds and let it crackle.
10. Add crushed green chilli-ginger and saute well.
11. Add potato-peas and saute well.
12. Add salt, turmeric, coriander and garam masala powder. Mix well and saute for 5 minutes.
13. Add amchur powder and chopped cilantro. Mix well and remove to a bowl.
14. To make samosa:
15. Take the kneaded dough and divide into 4 equal portions.
16. Take one portion and place on a dusted working surface. Roll it into a circle.
17. Make cut in the middle of the circle to get 2 semi circles. Apply some water over the edges.
18. Take one semi circle and join the edges to make a cone.
19. Stuff 2-3 teaspoons of prepared filling in the cone and seal the edges.
20. Heat oil for deep frying. Slide 2-3 prepared cones in the hot oil and deep fry until golden brown from both sides.
21. Drain excess oil and serve samosa hot
