Thai Green Vegetable Curry

Ingredients
• 2-3 Tbsp. oil
• 1-2 green chilies
• 1/4 cup onion
• 3 cloves garlic
• 1 thumb-size piece ginger
• 1 cup vegetable stock
• 2 Tbsp. soy sauce
• 1 Tbsp. ground coriander
• 2 tsp. ground cumin
• 2 kaffir lime leaves
• 1 loose cup finely chopped fresh coriander leaves and stems
• 1 sliced potato
• handful green beans
• 1 small bell pepper, red or green, sliced
• 1 head broccoli
• 1 carrot, sliced
• 1/4 cup fresh basil
Method
1. Heat a medium to large pot or wok over high heat. Drizzle in oil and add the lemongrass, chilies, onion, garlic and ginger. Stir-fry 1-2 minutes, or until onion has softened.
2. Add the stock, fish sauce or soy sauce, dry spices (coriander and cumin), kaffir lime leaves or lime zest, fresh coriander and 1/2 can coconut milk.
3. Now add sweet potato, green beans and carrot. When curry reaches a boil, reduce to medium-low heat, enough to get a good simmer going. Simmer 5-8 minutes, or until potato can be pierced through with a fork
