Chilly potato

Ingredients
• 5 sliced Potatoes
• 3 tablespoon Cornflour
• 1 tablespoon White pepper
• 2 tbsps Oil
• 2 sliced Onions
• 2 tablespoon chopped Garlic
• 4 Green chillies
• 2 teaspoon Soy sauce
• 1 teaspoon Vinegar
Method
1.Add potatoes in a bowl. Add corn flour, salt, and white pepper powder. Mix well and set aside for twenty minutes.
2.Heat two tablespoons oil in a non stick pan. Add onion roundels and sauté for two minutes or until pink. Add garlic, garlic paste and sauté for half minute.
3.Heat oil in a kadai. Deep fry the potatoes till crisp. Drain on an absorbent paper and add to the onion-garlic mixture.
4.Add green chillies, soy sauce and MSG and mix well. Check the seasonings. Add vinegar and mix well.Serve hot
