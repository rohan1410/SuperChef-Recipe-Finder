French Rice

Ingredients
• Rice - 2 cups
• Onions -2
• Carrots -3
• Capsicums -2
• Tomatoes -2
• Green chillies -2 (Sliced)
• Pepper powder - 1/2 tsp
• Butter -50 gms
• Salt to taste
Method
1. Wash the rice and soak in water for 20 minutes. Add 3 1/2 cups of water and cook till half and keep aside.
2. Dice all the vegetables and chop tomatoes, onions finely.
3. Heat butter in a frying pan, add onions and fry till soft. Add diced vegetables and stir fry for few minutes./td>
4. Take baking dish, grease it with butter and put half cooked rice and fried vegetables in it.
5. Pour little water and salt, pepper powder, green chillies.
6. Bake in a moderate oven for 15-20 minutes. Serve hot with chinese sauce or tomato sauce.
