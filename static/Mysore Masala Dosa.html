Mysore Masala Dosa

Ingredients
• 2 cups dosa batter
• 1 and ½ cup potato sabzi for the dosa
• 1 and ½ cup chopped onion
• 1 and ½ cup chopped tomato
• 1 and ½ cup chopped green bell pepper, carrots, beetroot, cabbage
• pav bhaji masala as required
• 2 tbsp red chili powder
• 1 bowl coconut chutney as an accompaniment
Method
1. spread a large spoonful or ladle of dosa batter on a hot tava or frying pan.
2. add some oil and let the dosa cook for 1-2 minutes.
3. now add the potato sabzi/masala.
4. add the chopped veggies.
5. sprinkle the pav bhaji masala & salt.
6. add some more oil or butter.
7. with a potato masher mash the whole mixture well.
8. cover the dosa from both sides or just from one side.
9. flip and cook the dosa from both the sides.
10. serve mysore masala dosa hot with coconut chutney and veg sambar.
