Mushroom pepper fry

Ingredients
• 2 tablespoon coconut oil
• ½ teaspoon mustard seeds
• 92 grams onion
• ½ sliced garlic
• 200 grams mushroom
• 115 grams capsicum
• 1 green chili
• 9 to 10 curry leaves (chopped)
• 1 tablespoon black pepper
• 1 teaspoon coriander powder
• ½ teaspoon cumin powder
• a pinch of turmeric powder
• 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
Method
1. heat 2 tablespoons coconut oil in a pan.
2. add ½ teaspoon mustard seeds and let them crackle.
3. after the mustard seeds crackle, then add 1 cup thinly sliced onions.
4. mix the onions very well and saute on a low to medium flame, till they start to turn light golden.
5. then add ½ teaspoon ginger-garlic paste and saute till the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away.
6. now add the sliced mushrooms.
7. mix very well and saute them on a medium flame.
8. first the mushrooms will release a lot of water.
9. continue to saute till all the water has evaporated and the mushrooms are almost cooked.
10. then add 1 cup sliced bell peppers and mix well.
11. saute the capsicum slices till they are half cooked. they should have some crunch in them.
12. then add 1 green chili, sliced or chopped and 2 teaspoons chopped curry leaves and mix well.
13. now add the coarsely powdered black pepper and fennel seeds along with ½ teaspoon coriander powder, ½ teaspoon cumin powder and a pinch of turmeric powder.
14. mix all the spices very well with the mushroom and capsicum mixture.
15. season with salt.
16. mix again and then switch off the flame. check the taste and add more salt if required.
17. add 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves. stir.
18. serve mushroom pepper fry hot with chapatis or rotis
